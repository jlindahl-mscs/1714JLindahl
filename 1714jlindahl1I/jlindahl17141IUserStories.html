Neighborhood Book Share Database User Stories

1714 Joslyn Lindahl

Description of Database

My database will be a neighborhood book share program. The database will used to catalog what books the people in your neighborhood have to share and are willing to share with eachother.

To prevent confusion a lender is someone who has the book that can be borrowed. A borrower is the person borrowing the book.

User Stories

    As a neighborhood member, I want to borrow and share books with others within the neighborhood.
    As a borrower, I want to see what books are available to borrow in my neighborhood.
    As a lender, I want to keep track of who has borrowed my book and when it might be returned.
    As a borrower, I want to search for books by title, author, ISBN, and genre.
    As a lender and a borrower, I want to be able to contact another lender or borrower that has possession of a certain book.
